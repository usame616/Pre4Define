We are Pre4Define!

We are 4 students from Bogazici University. One of our team members is a game developer who is publishing games on Google Play Store and one of the obstacles he came across is picking the color palette and what should be the theme of the game. So we came up with the idea of making a program to give suggestions to developers who have the same problem with our friend. After using our app, none of the developers will have a hard time picking the color theme on their game or selecting the true words for better attraction.
